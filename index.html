The Maison

A Legacy of
Unmatched Brilliance

Dongleer was born from a singular obsession: to create jewellery so extraordinary, it stops time. Founded by master craftsmen with roots in Europe's finest ateliers, we have spent decades refining our art — selecting only stones of the highest provenance, setting them with a precision that borders on the poetic.

Every stone we select carries within it the weight of centuries — we merely give it its final, perfect home.

Our commitment extends beyond beauty. We believe fine jewellery should be as transparent as the diamonds we set. Every piece comes with its full story: where the stone was born, how it was graded, and the hands that shaped it into something eternal.

The Art of Creation

Masterful Craftsmanship

Four sacred stages. One uncompromising standard. The Dongleer way.

I
Stone Selection

Our master gemologist travels to the world's finest sources, hand-selecting only stones that meet Dongleer's exacting standards for colour, clarity, and character.

II
Design & Blueprint

Every piece begins as a sketch and evolves through meticulous modelling, ensuring each design element works in perfect harmony with the stone it will hold.

III
Master Setting

Our artisans — each with no fewer than twenty years of experience — set each stone by hand, guided by instinct honed over a lifetime in the craft.

IV
Final Perfection

Before leaving our atelier, every piece undergoes a rigorous 47-point inspection. Only then does it receive the Dongleer hallmark: our lifetime guarantee.
